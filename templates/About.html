About This Project

This project is about Smart Urban Mobility and Futuristic Traffic Sustainability. The main goal is to make city travel easier, faster, and eco-friendly by using modern technologies such as smart traffic systems, electric vehicles, ride-sharing, data-driven planning. Our project focuses on reducing traffic jams, lowering pollution, and improving transport efficiency so that people can move safely and comfortably in urban areas.
